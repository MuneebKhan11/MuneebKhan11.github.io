My Journey

My passion for computer science began when I first learned to code in high school. I was fascinated by the endless possibilities that technology offered, and I quickly realized that I wanted to pursue a career in this field. I went on to study Computer Science at Nottingham Trent University, where I deepened my knowledge in various programming languages and technologies.

Experiences and Achievements

During my time at university, I completed several courses, where I gained hands-on experience in software development and project management. Additionally, I have worked on numerous personal and group projects, which have allowed me to further develop my skills in web development, data analysis, and artificial intelligence. Some of my most notable achievements include winning the 2022 Discord Hackathon.

Motivation

My motivation to continually grow and learn as a computer scientist comes from my desire to create innovative solutions to real-world problems. I believe that technology has the power to transform lives and positively impact the world, and I am driven by the opportunity to contribute to this process. By staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and continually expanding my skill set, I hope to make a meaningful impact through my work and inspire others to do the same.
